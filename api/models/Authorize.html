Authorize

final class Authorize extends Authorization

A specific authorization by one party of another. An authorization represents granting of certain permissions to a party by/under another, which can also be viewed as group membership. Unlike most [TableRow] classes, these may be constructed directly, and thus are not expected to reflect the current state of the database in the same way and have different update semantics.
